So what is FOSS4G UK all about?

It's about welcoming everyone to the world of Open Source Geospatial

...be that newcomers and beginners

...people wanting to level up

...hard-core geeks and coders

...and people just looking for the right tools to get the job done

Software of any kind, and the community around it, can be intimidating, frustrating, and opaque

Flickr: Dushan Hanuska CC BY-SA 2.0

So we want to make it that bit easier, and more enjoyable

Flickr: Giusi Barbiani CC BY-SA 2.0

Either to learn something new,

find the tools you want,

Get involved in the community,

or even to contribute something back
